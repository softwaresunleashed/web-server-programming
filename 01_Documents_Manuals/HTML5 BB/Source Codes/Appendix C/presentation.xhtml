The b element: Allows you to bold your text.

The big element: Allows you to increase the font size of your text.

The hr element: Displays an horizontal line.

The i element: Allows you to italicize your text.

The small element: Allows you to decrease the font size of your text.

The sub element: Allows you to display subscripted text.

The sup element: Allows you to display superscripted text.

The tt element: Allows you to display text as teletype or monospaced .
